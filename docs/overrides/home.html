Think of KubeStellar as a post office for your Kubernetes resources. Like a post office, KubeStellar doesn't open your packages; it safely stores and delivers them to selected clusters worldwide—public clouds, private clouds, or at the edge. It's a super useful solution for spreading your Kubernetes resources wherever you need them. Don't change anything, just add KubeStellar!
